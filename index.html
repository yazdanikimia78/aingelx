How is this different from a generic email template?
Templates are written for everyone, which means they resonate with no one. Every Aingelx sequence is written specifically for your industry, your offer, and your target customer — from scratch every time.
What types of businesses does this work for?
Any service-based business that follows up with leads. This includes consultants, agencies, real estate professionals, healthcare providers, SaaS companies, and more. If you have customers to reach, we can write for you.
Do I need any special tools or software?
For the Starter plan, any email client works — Gmail, Outlook, Apple Mail. For the Done For You plan, we'll configure everything directly in whatever tool you already use, including CRMs and marketing platforms.
How quickly will I receive my sequence?
Starter orders are delivered within 48 hours. Done For You orders receive priority treatment and are typically delivered within 24 hours. You'll receive a confirmation email immediately after ordering.
What if I'd like changes to my sequence?
The Done For You plan includes one full revision round. If you'd like adjustments to the Starter plan, we offer revisions at a flat rate. Our goal is for you to be completely satisfied with the result.
Is my information kept confidential?
Absolutely. All business information you share with us is kept strictly confidential and is never shared with third parties. We take your privacy seriously.
